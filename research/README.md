## Key Findings

- **No two European countries use the same rating system** (COST TU1406 finding) — DeepInspect's 11-criteria framework is more comprehensive than any single national system
- **Inspector variability is the #1 problem** — AI consistency is immediately valuable even at lower accuracy than the best human
- **Scour causes 47-53% of U.S. bridge collapses** (Wardhana & Hadipriono 2003; Water 2023 review) but is under-monitored between inspection cycles — DeepInspect addresses this gap
- **~300,000 EU bridges are 50+ years old** with €10B+ annual maintenance deficit — the market need is enormous
- **No AI system has regulatory approval to replace physical inspection** — DeepInspect is correctly positioned as screening/prioritization
- **DeepInspect's unique niche**: zero-cost, city-scale, physics-grounded triage with explicit confidence bounds and field-inspection flags
